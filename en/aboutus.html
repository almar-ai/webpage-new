Meet Almar AI

Artificial Intelligence built with precision, vision, and trust.

Two brothers, one vision: turning data into intelligent decisions

Almar AI was founded by two brothers who share a strong technical background and a common goal: to apply artificial intelligence in a practical, transparent, and impactful way for modern businesses.

With experience at BNP Paribas, Vodafone, and EY, we bring together aerospace and telecommunications engineering with real-world expertise in data science, machine learning, and the deployment of intelligent systems into production.

From predictive models to natural language processing, computer vision, or Langchain-powered AI agents, we build custom solutions tailored to each client—always with a focus on explainability, scalability, and measurable results.

We operate 100% remotely, with a base in Madrid and the flexibility to meet in person when required. Our commitment is clear: to deliver value through technical excellence, professional integrity, and straightforward communication.

Almar AI is innovation driven by science—and trust built between brothers.
